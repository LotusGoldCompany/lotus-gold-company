How should we structure the new experience?

Both options will be fully mobile-optimized and high-conversion

1

The "Elite Single-Page"

A seamless, high-performance flow. Services are detailed sections on the homepage. Best for immediate trust and fast booking.

Pros

  • Faster to build & maintain
  • Higher mobile conversion rate
  • Unified brand storytelling

Cons

  • Limited SEO depth per service
  • Can feel crowded if content grows
2

The "Authority Brand" Expansion

Dedicated pages for each service (e.g. /gold-loan-release). Better for ranking in Google for specific local keywords.

Pros

  • Maximum Local SEO visibility
  • More room for specialized trust badges
  • Feels like a larger enterprise

Cons

  • More complex navigation
  • Higher maintenance cost
